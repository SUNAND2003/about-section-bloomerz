About Us

Founded in 2003, Marine Spare Parts has been the
trusted partner for maritime professionals worldwide,
providing high-quality spare parts and components for vessels of all sizes.


Our comprehensive inventory includes generators,
pumps, valves, engines, navigation equipment, and
safety systems from leading manufacturers.


We understand the critical nature of
marine operations and the importance of
reliable, certified components. With over
two decades of experience, we've built
lasting relationships with shipowners,
operators, and maintenance teams across
six continents, ensuring vessels stay
operational and crews remain safe at sea.
